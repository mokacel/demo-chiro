## We treat the root cause

Most clinics treat the symptom. We're interested in why it's happening — and making sure it doesn't come back. That means taking time with each patient, understanding how they move, work, and live, and building a care plan around the actual problem.

## The team

**Dr. Elena Rossi, DC** — Lead chiropractor with 12 years in clinical practice. Specialises in sports injury, postural correction, and prenatal care. Trained at Palmer College of Chiropractic.

**James Okafor, RMT** — Registered massage therapist with a background in sports medicine. Works with everyone from desk workers to endurance athletes.

## Our approach

We don't do long treatment packages you didn't ask for. First visit is a full assessment. From there we give you an honest picture of what we're working with and how long it's likely to take. Most patients see meaningful improvement within 4–6 sessions.
